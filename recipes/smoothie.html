Green Smoothie Recipe

Description:

This is the best spinach smoothie that's so tasty, you'll forget that you're eating your greens. This tasty smoothie can be made two ways: with cashews for some protein, or with more fruit to keep it nut-free.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Spinach
  • 1 Green Apple
  • 1/4 cup of Cashews
  • 1 tbps Lemon Juice
  • Ice
  • 1/2 cup of Water

Directions

  1. Chop the apple into chunks
  2. Place everything in a blender
  3. Blend until smooth, stopping and scrapping as necessary.

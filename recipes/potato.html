Air Fried Sweet Potato

Description

A baked sweet potato in the air fryer comes out beautifully crusty on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside, just what a great baked potato should be! Serve them with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potato
  • 1 tbsp avocado or olive oil
  • Chives
  • 1/2 tsp coarse sea salt

Directions

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Brush potatoes with oil of choice, sprinkle with salt, and place them in the air fryer basket.
  3. Cook potatoes until very tender when pierced with a fork, about 1 hour.
  4. Add chives when they are done!
